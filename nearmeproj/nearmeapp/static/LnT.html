Larsen & Toubro Pvt Ltd





​Larsen & Toubro Limited (L&T) is a prominent Indian multinational conglomerate headquartered in Mumbai. Established in 1938 by Danish engineers Henning Holck-Larsen and Søren Kristian Toubro, the company has evolved into a global leader in engineering, construction, manufacturing, and financial services. Operating in over 50 countries, L&T is renowned for its expertise in executing large-scale infrastructure projects, including transportation systems, power plants, and water treatment facilities. The company's diverse portfolio encompasses sectors such as technology, defense, and real estate, with subsidiaries like L&T Technology Services and LTIMindtree extending its reach into IT and digital solutions. L&T's commitment to innovation and quality has solidified its position as a key player in driving India's industrial and economic growth. ​
